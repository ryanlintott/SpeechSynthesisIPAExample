# FB23041286 - IPA Pronunciations are broken

Playing the same IPA pronunciations on iOS 26.5 and iOS 27.0 beta 1 yield very different results. It appears as if iOS is ignoring the IPA symbols.

Open the app and press play (on a real iOS device only) and it should say the word “blend” using the IPA pronunciation “blɛnd”. On iOS 27 it instead says “B L N D”. This is the same for many other IPA pronunciations as it appears process them as regular English words.

[This video](https://mastodon.social/@ryanlintott/116727412955851360) shows the Old English Wordhord app (the current App Store version) on a phone with iOS 26.5 on the left and iOS 27.0 beta 1 on the right.
I use IPA pronunciations on thousands of words in my app so I would really like this fixed before iOS 27 is released.
